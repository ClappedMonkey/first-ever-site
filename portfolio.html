Creating a Logo

This post was written by ClappedMonkey

The themometer idea was a flop because we found out that if the temperature outside the house was to be warm then the temperature in the house would be cold. So we decided to remove that feature of the project. The logo I created is shown below. If you want a logo created for you just send me an email.
